Official Program Information

Unlocking Market Access

"Unlocking market access for industrial, solid minerals and commodity potentials"

June 29 – July 4, 2026
Karmo District, Abuja

Core Mission

The ICAI Trade Fair 2026 is a strategic international business festival designed to promote industrial development and commercialization of local innovations in Nigeria's solid minerals and commodity sectors.

Accelerate Industrialization

Creating national awareness regarding vast natural resources, technological innovations, and investment prospects.

Showcase Innovations

An international platform for exhibitors to display cutting-edge products and manufacturing technologies.

Bridge the Gap

Connecting research and innovation with commercialization by providing a specialized global marketplace.

Strengthen Collaboration

Enhancing working relationships between the public sector, private corporations, and development partners.

Strategic Partners

In collaboration with leading Federal MDAs, International Trade Bodies, and Strategic Industrial Partners.

Thematic Pillars

1 Nation-building & Growth
2 Global Market Integration
3 Digital Transformation
4 startup Ecosystems
5 Ethics & Governance
6 Inclusive Sustainability

Focus Sectors

Solid Minerals

Processed minerals, intermediates, and ceramics/glass inputs supporting downstream manufacturing.

Agriculture

Agrotechnology, rural vitalization, and essential commodity value chains.

Manufacturing

Industrial machinery, chemicals, EVs, and smart mobility solutions.

Energy & Metals

Battery materials, energy storage storage, and metal-based industrial value chains.

Empowering industries through awareness, commercialization, and global partnerships.
