Who We Are

Our Profile

A forward-thinking organization committed to fostering industrial development and promoting commercialization opportunities.

Bridging Innovation & Market

Industrial and Commercialization Awareness Initiative (ICAI) Ltd/GTE is dedicated to bridging the gap between innovative ideas and sustainable industrial practices.

At ICAI, we focus on raising awareness about the importance of industrialization and commercialization as critical drivers of economic growth. We achieve this by organizing workshops, training programs, and community engagement initiatives designed to educate stakeholders about best practices, emerging trends, and strategic opportunities.

Our Goal

To promote sustainable industrial development and empower entrepreneurs by bridging the gap between innovation, production, and market access.

Target Audience

We empower a diverse ecosystem of stakeholders to drive national economic transformation.

SMEs

Existing businesses looking to scale operations, access markets, or adopt industrial best practices.

Innovators

Individuals or groups developing new ideas, seeking support in commercialization and market entry.

Startups

Aspiring entrepreneurs requiring training, mentorship, and funding for business ventures.

Govt Agencies

Ministries and departments focusing on industrial development, trade, and entrepreneurship.

Community Orgs

Groups promoting local entrepreneurship, skills development, and industrial awareness.

Private Sector

Large corporations seeking partnerships for innovation and supply chain integration.

Critical Stakeholders

We work in lockstep with Nigeria's most influential governing bodies and industrial leaders to ensure a knowledgeable and skilled society equipped to excel in the global economy.

Empowering industries through awareness, commercialization, and global partnerships.
