Strategic Document

Trade Fair Business Plan

Repositioning Nigeria's industrial products from raw exports to value-added assets.

Project Objectives

  • Showcase commodities from all states in a unified marketplace.
  • Promote beneficiation and MSME startups for local content.
  • Link producers to buyers, investors, and exporters via B2B/B2G deals.
  • Drive non-oil exports and AfCFTA integration.

Target Audience

FMITI Solid Mineral Producers Industrial Manufacturers Foreign Buyers Financial Institutions Commodity Associations Diplomatic Missions

Financial Outlook

Projected Revenue ₦ 1,150,000,000

Risk Management

Mitigating low turnout via aggressive marketing, hybrid event options, and strategic partnerships with industry influencers and government bodies.
