The Mandate

About Us

Driving Nigeria's transition from a consumption-based to a production-driven economy.

A Catalyst for Industrial Transformation

Industrial and Commercialization Awareness Initiatives (ICAI) serves as the primary bridge between Nigeria's untapped industrial potential and global market opportunities.

Our mandate is built on the necessity of commercializing homegrown innovations and showcasing Nigeria's industrial capacity to the international community. Throuhg strategic partnerships with government agencies like FMITI, we catalyze growth in Solid Minerals, Agriculture, and Manufacturing.

17+ Governing MDAs
36 States Represented

Strategic Pillars

Investment Mobilization

Identifying and securing global investments for Nigerian industrial ecosystems and infrastructure.

SME Readiness

Empowering MSMEs with the tools to bridge the gap between innovation and market access.

Global Offtake

Facilitating new supply and export leads through strategic international partnerships and AfCFTA.

Job Creation

Using industrialization as a vehicle for sustainable livelihood and national economic participation.

Core Objectives

01

Industrial Promotion

Promote industrialization by engaging in activities that support the development and enhancement of industrial sectors across the federation.

02

Commercialization & Growth

Facilitate commercialization of innovative products and services through R&D and partnerships with local and international businesses, ensuring advancements contribute to sustainable economic growth.

03

Capacity Building

Provide education and training programs aimed at equipping individuals and organizations with the necessary skills and knowledge to participate effectively in industrial activities.

04

Strategic Research

Conduct research in various fields related to industrialization and commercialization, disseminating findings to stakeholders to foster informed decision making and policy development.

05

Sustainable Practices

Promote sustainable practices in all industrial activities, ensuring that environmental considerations are integrated into all commercialization strategies.
